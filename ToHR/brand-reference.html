01 · Logo

The logo

Our logo is called the Held Drop: a hexagonal cell, open at the base, sheltering a single drop of water. No fire, no cross, no clinical iconography. It is shown here on white, the way it would appear in a research deck, a grant application, or printed on an install flyer at the gate.

The open base

The temple is entered, never sealed. The doorway is part of the mark: welcome stated in geometry.

The drop

Water on playa, the reagent drop in testing, the tear held in sanctuary. The most concrete act of care, made iconic.

The hexagon

The cell of the hive. Empty cells are the interface; the marked cell means Temple: verified human help.
